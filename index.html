about us

education

SK Children Foundation aims to teach needy children subjects and life values th at go beyond the horizon of normal schooling. Founded in 2016 by Mr. Raghav Sharma, the NGO has changed the lives of 2000+ people in need- spanning two centers in Janakpuri, New Delhi and Pune, Maharashtra. Classes are conducted for children free of cost 365 days a year in both the centers with the focus being on academics as well as extra curricular activities resulting in holistic development of the children.
